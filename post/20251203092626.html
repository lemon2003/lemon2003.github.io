1. 社会发展类

英文 中文释义 例句
aging population 老龄化人口 China is facing the challenge of an aging population. 中国正面临老龄化人口的挑战。
demographic structure 人口结构 The demographic structure​ of China has changed significantly. 中国的人口结构发生了显著变化。
social security 社会保障 The government is improving the social security​ system. 政府正在完善社会保障体系。
economic reform 经济改革 Economic reform​ has promoted rapid development. 经济改革促进了快速发展。
sustainable development 可持续发展 We should adhere to the concept of sustainable development. 我们应坚持可持续发展的理念。
urbanization 城市化 The pace of urbanization​ is accelerating. 城市化的步伐正在加快。
infrastructure 基础设施 The country invests heavily in infrastructure. 国家在基础设施上投入巨大。
livelihood 民生 The government pays great attention to issues related to people’s livelihood. 政府非常关注民生问题。
welfare 福利 People’s welfare​ has been significantly improved. 人民的福利得到了显著改善。
coverage 覆盖范围 The coverage​ of medical insurance is expanding. 医疗保险的覆盖范围正在扩大。
pension 养老金 Many elderly people rely on a pension​ after retirement. 许多老年人退休后依靠养老金生活。
medical insurance 医疗保险 Medical insurance​ reduces the burden of medical expenses. 医疗保险减轻了医疗费用的负担。
income distribution 收入分配 The government aims to optimize income distribution. 政府旨在优化收入分配。
poverty alleviation 扶贫 Poverty alleviation​ work has achieved remarkable results. 扶贫工作取得了显著成效。
social stability 社会稳定 Social stability​ is crucial for development. 社会稳定对发展至关重要。
urbanization rate 城镇化率 The urbanization rate​ continues to rise. 城镇化率持续上升。
housing condition 住房条件 People’s housing conditions​ have been greatly improved. 人民的住房条件得到了极大改善。
public service 公共服务 It is necessary to enhance the level of public services. 有必要提升公共服务的水平。
environmental protection 环保 Environmental protection​ is everyone’s responsibility. 环保是每个人的责任。
resource allocation 资源分配 Market plays a key role in resource allocation. 市场在资源分配中起关键作用。
technological innovation 科技创新 Technological innovation​ drives economic growth. 科技创新驱动经济增长。
quality of life 生活质量 The quality of life​ for urban residents is improving. 城市居民的生活质量正在提高。
social organization 社会组织 Social organizations​ actively participate in community services. 社会组织积极参与社区服务。
government subsidy 政府补贴 The project receives government subsidies. 该项目享有政府补贴。
policy implementation 政策实施 The key lies in the policy implementation. 关键在于政策实施。

2. 经济发展类

英文 中文释义 例句
economic growth 经济增长 China has maintained a high rate of economic growth. 中国保持了高速的经济增长。
GDP (Gross Domestic Product) 国内生产总值 GDP​ is an important indicator of economic strength. 国内生产总值是经济实力的重要指标。
industrialization 工业化 The process of industrialization​ is irreversible. 工业化的进程是不可逆的。
investment 投资 Foreign investment​ is encouraged in this area. 该领域鼓励外资投资。
consumption 消费 Domestic consumption​ is stimulating the economy. 国内消费正在刺激经济。
export 出口 The export​ of electronic products has increased. 电子产品出口增加了。
import 进口 China imports​ a large amount of crude oil each year. 中国每年进口大量原油。
inflation 通货膨胀 The government is taking measures to control inflation. 政府正在采取措施控制通货膨胀。
employment 就业 Stabilizing employment​ is a priority. 稳定就业是优先事项。
unemployment 失业 The unemployment​ rate remains low. 失业率保持低位。
enterprise 企业 Small and medium enterprises​ are vital to the economy. 中小企业对经济至关重要。
market economy 市场经济 China is developing a socialist market economy. 中国正在发展社会主义市场经济。
economic crisis 经济危机 The world faced a severe economic crisis​ in 2008. 2008年世界面临严重的经济危机。
financial system 金融体系 The financial system​ is being reformed. 金融体系正在改革中。
globalisation 全球化 Globalisation​ has both opportunities and challenges. 全球化既有机遇也有挑战。
trade deficit 贸易逆差 The country has a large trade deficit. 该国有巨额贸易逆差。
surplus 顺差 China has a trade surplus​ with many countries. 中国对许多国家有贸易顺差。
macroeconomic 宏观经济的 The government regulates from a macroeconomic​ perspective. 政府从宏观经济的角度进行调控。
infrastructure 基础设施 Investment in infrastructure​ is increasing. 对基础设施的投资正在增加。
purchasing power 购买力 The purchasing power​ of residents is growing. 居民的购买力不断增强。
economic recovery 经济复苏 Signs of economic recovery​ are emerging. 经济复苏的迹象正在显现。
industrial structure 产业结构 Adjusting the industrial structure​ is necessary. 调整产业结构是必要的。
sustainable development 可持续发展 We must adhere to sustainable development. 我们必须坚持可持续发展。
economic transformation 经济转型 The country is undergoing economic transformation. 国家正处于经济转型期。
per capita 人均 Per capita​ income has risen. 人均收入增加了。

porcelain 瓷器 Chinese porcelain​ is famous worldwide. 中国瓷器世界闻名。
cultural exchange 文化交流 Cultural exchange​ promotes mutual understanding. 文化交流促进相互理解。
higher education 高等教育 Higher education​ in China is developing rapidly. 中国的高等教育发展迅速。
vocational education 职业教育 Vocational education​ trains skilled talents. 职业教育培养技能型人才。
literacy rate 识字率 The literacy rate​ in China is very high. 中国的识字率很高。
compulsory education 义务教育 Compulsory education​ lasts for nine years in China. 中国实行九年义务教育。

curriculum 课程 The school offers a rich curriculum. 学校提供丰富的课程。

6. 景点建筑与旅游专题词汇

英文 中文释义 例句
1. be situated/located in 位于，坐落于 The Forbidden City is situated in​ the center of Beijing. 故宫位于北京市中心。
2. historical site 历史遗址 This temple is a protected historical site. 这座寺庙是受保护的历史遗址。
3. scenic spot 景点 The West Lake is a famous scenic spot​ in Hangzhou. 西湖是杭州著名的景点。
4. places of interest 名胜古迹 The city is famous for its many places of interest. 这座城市以其众多名胜古迹而闻名。
5. attract tourists 吸引游客 The beautiful landscape attracts tourists​ from all over the world. 美丽的风景吸引着来自世界各地的游客。
6. architecture 建筑 The unique architecture​ reflects the local culture. 独特的建筑反映了当地文化。
7. ancient architecture 古建筑 The palace is a masterpiece of ancient architecture. 这座宫殿是古建筑的杰作。
8. cultural heritage 文化遗产 The Great Wall is a world cultural heritage​ site. 长城是世界文化遗产。
9. historical and cultural value 历史与文化价值 These relics have great historical and cultural value. 这些文物具有巨大的历史与文化价值。
10. date back to 追溯到，始建于 The temple dates back to​ the Tang Dynasty. 这座寺庙始建于唐朝。
11. with a history of … years 有…年的历史 It is a city with a history of​ 3,000 years. 这是一座有着3000年历史的城市。
12. symbolize 象征 The bridge symbolizes​ the friendship between the two cities. 这座桥象征着两座城市之间的友谊。
13. reputation 声誉，名望 It enjoys a high reputation​ for its natural beauty. 它因其自然美景而享有盛誉。
14. landmark 地标 The Oriental Pearl TV Tower is a landmark​ of Shanghai. 东方明珠电视塔是上海的一个地标。
15. picturesque 风景如画的 The village is famous for its picturesque​ scenery. 这个村庄以其风景如画的景色而闻名。
16. magnificent 宏伟的，壮丽的 Visitors are amazed by the magnificent​ palace. 游客们对这座宏伟的宫殿感到惊叹。
17. well-preserved 保存完好的 This is a well-preserved​ ancient city. 这是一座保存完好的古城。
18. restoration 修复 The restoration​ of the old building took three years. 对这座旧建筑的修复花了三年时间。
19. opening hours 开放时间 Please check the opening hours​ before your visit. 请于参观前查看开放时间。
20. admission fee/ticket 门票 The admission fee​ for the museum is very reasonable. 博物馆的门票价格非常合理。
21. tourist attraction 旅游景点 It has become a popular tourist attraction. 它已成为一个热门的旅游景点。
22. guide 导游；指南 We hired a guide​ to show us around. 我们雇了一名导游带我们参观。
23. cultural relics 文物 The museum houses many precious cultural relics. 该博物馆收藏了许多珍贵的文物。
24. inscription 碑文，题字 There are ancient inscriptions​ on the stone tablet. 石碑上有古老的碑文。
25. emperor 皇帝 The palace was the home of the emperor. 这座宫殿曾是皇帝的居所。
26. dynasty 朝代 The building was constructed during the Ming Dynasty. 这座建筑建于明朝**。
27. temple 寺庙 Many people visit the temple​ to pray. 许多人到这座寺庙祈福。
28. palace 宫殿 The Potala Palace​ is located in Lhasa. 布达拉宫位于拉萨。
29. garden 园林 The garden​ is famous for its unique design. 这座园林以其独特的设计而闻名。
30. landscape 景观，风景 The landscape​ of Guilin is breathtaking. 桂林的风景美得令人窒息。
31. natural scenery 自然风光 Tourists come here to enjoy the natural scenery. 游客来这里欣赏自然风光。
32. breathtaking 令人惊叹的 The view from the top is breathtaking. 从顶部看到的景色令人惊叹。
33. peak 山峰 We finally reached the mountain peak. 我们终于到达了山峰。
34. cave 洞穴 The cave​ is famous for its stalactites. 这个洞穴以其钟乳石而闻名。
35. waterfall 瀑布 The waterfall​ is 60 meters high. 这个瀑布有60米高。
36. lake 湖泊 The lake​ is as smooth as a mirror. 湖面平静如镜。
37. river 河流 The river​ runs through the city. 这条河流穿城而过。
38. cruise 乘船游览 We took a cruise​ on the Li River. 我们乘船游览了漓江。
39. hiking 徒步 Hiking​ is a popular activity in the mountains. 徒步是山里一项流行的活动。
40. cable car 缆车 We took the cable car​ to the top of the mountain. 我们乘坐缆车到达山顶。
41. souvenir 纪念品 I bought a souvenir​ to remember the trip. 我买了一个纪念品来纪念这次旅行。
42. local delicacy 当地美食 Don’t miss the local delicacies. 不要错过当地美食。
43. handicraft 手工艺品 The village is famous for its handicrafts. 这个村庄以其手工艺品而闻名。
44. folk custom 民俗 Tourists are interested in the folk customs​ here. 游客对这里的民俗很感兴趣。
45. bustling 热闹的，熙熙攘攘的 The bustling​ street is full of shops and restaurants. 这条熙熙攘攘的街道上遍布商店和餐馆。
46. tranquil/peaceful 宁静的 The park is a tranquil​ escape from the city. 这个公园是一个远离城市的宁静之所。
47. crowded 拥挤的 The scenic spot can get very crowded​ during holidays. 这个景点在节假日会非常拥挤。
48. itinerary 行程，旅行计划 We have a tight itinerary​ for our two-day trip. 我们为期两天的旅行行程很紧。
49. accommodation 住宿 We have booked our accommodation​ online. 我们已在网上预订了住宿。
50. transportation 交通 Public transportation​ to the scenic area is convenient. 前往景区的公共交通很方便。

7.连接词

类别 英文 中文释义 典型例句（结合真题语境）
开头与引出话题​ 1. It is well-known that… 众所周知… It is well-known that​ China has a long history and brilliant culture. 众所周知，中国拥有悠久的历史和灿烂的文化。
2. With the development of… 随着…的发展 With the rapid development of​ economy, people’s living standards have improved. 随着经济的快速发展，人民生活水平提高了。
3. In recent years 近年来 In recent years, online shopping has become increasingly popular. 近年来，网上购物变得越来越流行。
表示顺序与列举​ 4. First and foremost 首先，最重要的是 First and foremost, we should protect the environment. 首先，我们应保护环境。
5. Furthermore / Moreover 此外，而且 The park is beautiful. Furthermore, it is free to the public. 这个公园很美，而且对公众免费开放。
6. Last but not least 最后但同样重要的是 Last but not least, we should thank our volunteers. 最后但同样重要的是，我们应感谢志愿者。
7. On the one hand… on the other hand 一方面…另一方面 On the one hand, cars bring convenience. On the other hand, they cause pollution. 一方面，汽车带来便利；另一方面，它们造成污染。
表示递进与补充​ 8. not only… but also… 不但…而且… The project is not only​ profitable but also​ environmentally friendly. 这个项目不但有利可图，而且环保。
9. In addition / Additionally 另外 In addition, we need to consider the cost. 另外，我们需要考虑成本。
10. What’s more 更重要的是 The food is delicious. What’s more, the price is very reasonable. 食物很美味，更重要的是，价格很合理。
表示对比与转折​ 11. However / Nevertheless 然而，但是 The task is difficult. However, we will not give up. 任务很艰巨，但是我们不会放弃。
12. While / Whereas 而（表示对比） While​ the south is rich, the north is developing fast. 南方很富裕，而北方正在快速发展。
13. On the contrary 相反地 It wasn’t a bad thing. On the contrary, it was a good lesson. 这不是坏事，相反，这是一个很好的教训。
14. Despite / In spite of 尽管 Despite​ the heavy rain, they continued working. 尽管下着大雨，他们仍继续工作。
表示因果与结果​ 15. Therefore / Thus / Hence 因此，所以 The weather was bad. Therefore, the flight was delayed. 天气很糟，因此航班延误了。
16. As a result / Consequently 结果 He practiced very hard. As a result, he won the championship. 他训练非常刻苦，结果赢得了冠军。
17. so… that… / such… that… 如此…以致于… The scenery was so​ beautiful that​ we lost ourselves in it. 风景如此美丽，以致于我们沉醉其中。
18. contribute to / lead to 导致，有助于 Hard work contributes to​ success. 努力有助于成功。
19. result in / result from 导致 / 由…引起 The accident resulted from​ careless driving. 事故由粗心驾驶引起。
表示举例与解释​ 20. For example / For instance 例如 China has many famous rivers, for example, the Yangtze River. 中国有许多著名的河流，例如长江。
21. Such as 比如，诸如 I like outdoor activities, such as​ hiking and cycling. 我喜欢户外活动，比如徒步和骑行。
22. In other words 换句话说 He is very thrifty. In other words, he doesn’t like to waste money. 他很节俭，换句话说，他不喜欢浪费钱。
23. Take… for example 以…为例 Take​ the Great Wall for example, it is a symbol of China. 以长城为例，它是中国的象征。
表示条件与假设​ 24. If / Provided that 如果 If​ we protect the environment, the Earth will be better. 如果我们保护环境，地球会变得更好。
25. As long as 只要 As long as​ you work hard, you will succeed. 只要你努力，就会成功。
26. Otherwise 否则 We must act now, otherwise​ it will be too late. 我们必须现在行动，否则就太晚了。
表示时间与过程​ 27. Gradually / Step by step 逐渐地/一步步地 The city is gradually​ becoming more modern. 这座城市逐渐地变得更加现代化。
28. Meanwhile / At the same time 同时 He was working. Meanwhile, his wife was cooking. 他在工作，同时，他的妻子在做饭。
29. Previously / In the past 以前，在过去 In the past, communication was not so convenient. 在过去，通讯没有这么方便。
30. Currently / At present 目前，现在 At present, artificial intelligence is developing rapidly. 目前，人工智能正在快速发展。
31. So far / Up to now 到目前为止 So far, we have made great progress. 到目前为止，我们取得了巨大进展。
表示程度与范围​ 32. More and more 越来越多的 More and more​ people are paying attention to health. 越来越多的人开始关注健康。
33. A growing number of 日益增长的 A growing number of​ tourists are visiting China. 日益增长的游客来中国旅游。
34. The vast majority of 绝大多数 The vast majority of​ citizens support the policy. 绝大多数市民支持这项政策。
35. To some extent / In a way 在某种程度上 To some extent, technology has changed our lives. 在某种程度上，科技改变了我们的生活。
36. Especially / Particularly 尤其，特别 I love music, especially​ classical music. 我热爱音乐，特别是古典音乐。
表示总结与结论​ 37. In conclusion / To sum up 总之，总而言之 In conclusion, we should take action immediately. 总之，我们应立即采取行动。
38. In a word / In short 简而言之 In a word, the trip was fantastic. 简而言之，这次旅行棒极了。
39. All in all 总的来说 All in all, it was a successful conference. 总的来说，这是一次成功的会议。
40. From what has been discussed above 综上所述 From what has been discussed above, we can see its importance. 综上所述，我们可以看到它的重要性。
常用介词短语​ 41. According to 根据 According to​ the news, it will rain tomorrow. 根据新闻报道，明天会下雨。
42. Due to / Owing to 由于，因为 The match was canceled due to​ the bad weather. 比赛因天气不好被取消了。
43. In terms of 在…方面 China has made great achievements in terms of​ economic development. 中国在经济发展方面取得了巨大成就。
44. In order to 为了 In order to​ improve his English, he practices every day. 为了提高英语，他每天练习。
45. As for 至于，关于 As for​ the cost, we need to discuss it further. 至于费用，我们需要进一步讨论。
其他重要短语​ 46. It is said that… 据说… It is said that​ the castle is haunted. 据说那座城堡闹鬼。
47. There is no doubt that… 毫无疑问… There is no doubt that​ health is the most important. 毫无疑问，健康是最重要的。
48. It goes without saying that… 不言而喻的是… It goes without saying that​ water is essential for life. 不言而喻，水对生命是必需的。
49. …plays an important role in… 在…中扮演重要角色 The Internet plays an important role in​ our daily life. 互联网在我们的日常生活中扮演着重要角色。
50. …is considered (to be) / regarded as… …被认为是… The Great Wall is regarded as​ a symbol of China. 长城被认为是中国的象征。

如何高效使用这些词汇？

  • 替换低级词汇：将你常用的 and, but, so, also等，有意识地替换成 furthermore, however, therefore, in addition，文章会立刻显得更地道。

构建段落结构：

  • 开头：用 With the development of...或 In recent years...引出话题。

  • 中间：用 First..., Furthermore..., For example...来展开论述或举例。

    • 结尾：用 In conclusion...或 All in all...进行总结。
