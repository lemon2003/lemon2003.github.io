一、开头部分

开头第一句（描述背景或现象）

  1. With the remarkable development of advanced science and technology, an overwhelming majority of people acknowledged that … should not be underestimated.
    随着先进科学技术的显著发展，绝大多数人认为…不应该被低估。

  2. Along with the steady growth in the country’s economy as well as the people’s living standard, people attach more importance to…
    随着中国经济的稳步增长与人民生活水平的提高，人们越来越注重…

  3. In recent years, an increasing number of people have begun to challenge against the existing practice of…
    近年来，越来越多的人开始对…的现有说法提出质疑。

  4. An old saying goes that “Haste makes waste”, which mirrored a conspicuous standpoint that patience plays a significant role in our daily life.
    俗话说得好，“欲速则不达”，这反映了一个显而易见的观点，耐心在我们日常生活中扮演着十分重要的角色。

  5. In the contemporary society, with the help of translation apps, the tremendous quantity of individuals find it convenient to read foreign literature.
    当今社会，在翻译APP的帮助下，很多人发现阅读外国文学变得十分方便。

  6. Our society having progressed in such a striking rate, how to keep a healthy lifestyle has raised public awareness.
    我们的社会以如此惊人的速度发展着，如何保持健康的生活方式引起了公众的注意。

  7. An incremental number of green foods are making their appearance in our daily life and the vast overwhelming majority of people are becoming conscious of environmental protection.
    越来越多的绿色食品出现在我们日常生活中，并且绝大多数人正在意识到要保护环境。

开头第二句（强调主题重要性或作用）

  1. It can be characterized by an indispensable virtue that one needs to have in order to gain a strong foothold in society.
    它被认为是一个人立足于社会的必备品质。

  2. Innovation plays a significant and necessary role in our country’s construction and advancement.
    创新在国家的建设和进步中扮演着重要的角色。

  3. A high sense of responsibility has laid a solid foundation for the all-round development of individuals.
    高度的责任感为人的全面发展打下了坚实的基础。

  4. It goes without saying that keeping physically active is a fundamental approach to such a lifestyle.
    毫无疑问，保持这种生活方式的基本方式是进行体育活动。

  5. It’s universally acknowledged that only those with high sense of social responsibility can take the initiative to assume the responsibility of family and society.
    众所周知，只有那些具备高度社会责任感的人才能主动承担起家庭和社会的责任。

二、主体部分

主体段第一句（总括句）

  1. There may be a combination of multifarious interpretations can be conducive to certify it.
    结合一些例子有助于证实它。

  2. The reason why such a phenomenon emerges can be interpreted as follows.
    这种现象产生的原因如下。

  3. It’s arduous to identify the reasons for this phenomenon which it involves several complicated factors.
    很难找出这一现象的原因，因为它涉及了几个复杂的因素。

  4. People differ greatly in their attitudes toward this problem. Some people maintain that… Some people hold the opinion that…
    人们对于这个问题持不同的态度。一些人认为…，另一些人认为…

  5. … covers many aspects of the life.
    …包括了我们生活的方方面面。

  6. In the first place, curiosity is the premise of innovation, which can be seen as the key to success.
    首先，好奇心是创新的前提，而创新是成功的关键。

主体部分原因分析

  1. The deep root of this phenomenon lies in someone’s greed for money.
    这种现象的深层根源在于某人对金钱的贪婪。

  2. Another contributing force is that there are a large number of online stores, and consumers can choose the most satisfying product.
    另一个原因是网上商店数量庞大，消费者可以根据自己的需求选择最满意的产品。

  3. Quiet a considerable number of students study solely for the purpose of academic degree they would obtain upon graduation.
    有相当多的学生完全是出于拿到学位的动机学习的。

  4. Soaring car ownership compounds the chronic annoying problem, so we must sharply reduce the heavy reliance on cars and drive a shift to mass transportation.
    汽车拥有量激增也加剧了这个长期困扰我们的问题，因此，我们必须大幅减少对汽车的严重依赖，转而选择大众交通工具。

  5. And it is the key for individual success on the grounds that permanent persistence may boost our confidence, strengthen our will, and provide us with a second chance when we are in hardship.
    这是个人成功的关键，因为坚持可以提振我们的信心，增强我们的意志，在困境中为我们提供第二次成功的机会。

主体部分解释句

  1. A and B are interrelated and interdependent rather than be viewed as diametrically opposing force.
    A与B是相互联系与依赖的，而不应被视为对立的力量。

  2. Practice and diligence will bring you experience and inspiration, arouse your enthusiasm for study and help you to make progress every day.
    实践和勤奋努力将带给你经验/灵感，唤起你对学习的热情并帮助你每天有所进步。

  3. People sense to fail to take into account the fact that education does not end with graduation.
    人们似乎忽视了教育不应该随着毕业而结束这一事实。

  4. Those whose dreams become true have at least one thing in common, that is, they always hold fast to their dreams and always keep their feet on the ground.
    那些梦想成真的人至少有一个共同点，那就是他们总能坚持自己的梦想，总是脚踏实地。

主体部分举例

  1. The evidence that an incremental number of office works suffer from different types of illness demonstrate that exercise is supposed to give a priority.
    越来越多的办公职员患有不同种类的疾病表明锻炼应当被改在首位。

  2. Some people think that traditional classrooms replaced by network classroom will be an inexorable trend.
    一些人认为传统课堂被网络课堂取代会是一个必然趋势。

  3. When it comes to education, the majority of people believed that it’s a lifetime study.
    谈到教育，大部分人认为这是一个终身学习的过程。

  4. The latest survey show that quite a few children…
    最近的调查显示，不少的孩子…

  5. Imagine a adj student who is doing something, and he will be…
    设想一下一个什么样的学生正在做什么事情，他将会…

  6. There is no denying the fact that air pollution is an extremely serious problem: the municipal government should take efficient mechanisms to deal with it.
    无可否认，空气污染是个极其严重的问题，市政府应该采取有效的机制去解决它。

  7. An instance occured recently, in which one of my intimates who…
    最近发生的一个例子：我的一个好友…

主体部分态度观点

  1. It’s hard to overstate the significance of…
    怎么说…的重要性都不为过。

三、结尾部分

结尾第一句（得出结论）

  1. From what has been discussed above, we may safely draw the conclusion that…
    通过以上讨论，我们可以得出结论…

  2. Taking all factors into consideration, we can reasonably draw a conclusion that…
    考虑到所有因素，我们可以得出结论…

结尾第二句（未来展望/解决方案/做法）

  1. Therefore, we college students should take the initiative in response to the appeal for supplying help to children in poor regions.
    因此，我们大学生应主动响应为贫困地区儿童提供帮助的呼吁。

  2. According to the above analysis, we must bear in mind the significance of living a low-carbon lifestyle and then take effective action.
    根据以上分析，我们应该牢记低碳生活的重要性，然后采取有效行动。

  3. Quite a considerable number of students study solely for the purpose of academic degree they would obtain upon graduation.
    有相当多的学生完全是出于拿到学位的动机学习的。

  4. Soaring car ownership compounds the chronic annoying problem, so we must sharply reduce the heavy reliance on cars and drive a shift to mass transportation.
    汽车拥有量激增也加剧了这个长期困扰我们的问题，因此，我们必须大幅减少对汽车的严重依赖，转而选择大众交通工具。

  5. And it is the key for individual success on the grounds that permanent persistence may boost our confidence, strengthen our will, and provide us with a second chance when we are in hardship.
    这是个人成功的关键，因为坚持可以提振我们的信心，增强我们的意志，在困境中为我们提供第二次成功的机会。
